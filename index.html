This site is dedicated to three powerhouse Russian figure skaters under Coach Eteri Tutberidze.

Anna Scherbakova, Alena Kostornaia, and Alexandra Trusova—also known as 3A,Triple A, or the Russian Troika—have dominated ladies figure skating since 2018.

Troika

2: a group of three especially : an administrative or ruling body of three
